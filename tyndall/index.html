FAQ Section

Answers below are interactive and can be expanded with JavaScript for a cleaner browsing experience.

How do I start online banking?
Enroll through the official Tyndall online banking portal, verify your account details, and create secure login credentials. After activation, you can manage transfers, bill pay, and notifications from desktop or mobile channels listed in our Services page.

Where can I find branch and ATM addresses?
All listed branches and ATM guidance are consolidated on the local Locations page and mapped to the official source path: tyndall.org/learn/about-us/branch-and-atm-locations. This keeps address details easy to scan before visiting a location.

How can I contact Tyndall quickly?
Use toll-free (888) 896-3255, local (850) 769-9999, or the official contact portal at info.tyndall.org/contactus. Social channels are also provided in the footer on every page for updates and service announcements.

Is member money federally insured?
Yes. Tyndall Federal Credit Union deposits are federally insured by the NCUA up to applicable limits. The Bank page includes direct navigation to NCUA and related governance resources for members reviewing financial protection details.
